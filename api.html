    Measures¶

    Spike related measures and conversions, available in the measures sub-module.

    Measures¶

    Spike measures.

    compute_spike_rate(spikes)

    Estimate spike rate from a vector of spike times, in seconds.

    compute_isis(spikes)

    Compute inter-spike intervals.

    compute_cv(isis)

    Compute coefficient of variation.

    compute_fano_factor(spike_train)

    Compute the fano factor of a spike train.

    Conversions¶

    Conversions between spike representations.

    create_spike_train(spikes)

    Convert spike times into a binary spike train.

    convert_train_to_times(train)

    Convert a spike train representation into spike times, in milliseconds.

    convert_isis_to_spikes(isis[, offset, ...])

    Convert a sequence of inter-spike intervals to spike times.
